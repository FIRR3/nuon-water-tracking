Every 7 days, the app will expire and you'll need to rebuild it:
1. Connect your iPhone via USB to your Mac
2. Run: npx expo run:ios --Device

Between rebuilds, you can develop normally over WiFi with:
npm run dev
npx expo start --dev-client


For school computer:
# Build Android app (works on any OS)
eas build --profile development --platform android
# Or locally: npx expo run:android

# Then develop on your Asus
npx expo start --dev-client
# Connect your Android phone or use an emulator
